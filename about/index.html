About

History

The vision, concepts and goals were devised by João Costa starting in 2017.

In 2020, those personal projects were migrated to a virtual organization in order to create a community that shares the same vision of open systems.

Who we are

Organization to drive the development of open and standard system APIs.

Mission

Drive the development of standards to unlock the power of systems, to promote better interactions of Software and Hardware.
